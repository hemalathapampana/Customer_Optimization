# Customer Optimization - Brief Overview

## Section 1: What is Customer Optimization?

• **Purpose**: Automatically finds cheaper phone/data plans for company devices
• **How it works**: Analyzes actual usage and recommends cheapest plan that provides enough data
• **Example**: Company with 100 devices saves $2,250/month by switching from unlimited plans to right-sized plans
• **Benefit**: Significant cost savings without affecting device performance

## Section 2: How Cost Calculation Works

• **Data Collection**: System checks each device's monthly data usage and current plan costs
• **Plan Testing**: Tests every device on all available plans to find cheapest option
• **Cost Formula**: Base plan cost + overage charges = total monthly cost per device
• **Shared Pools**: Groups devices to share data allowances for additional savings
• **Example**: Device using 7GB - best option is 10GB plan ($45) vs unlimited ($60)

## Section 3: Complete Process Flow

• **Phase 1**: Customer requests optimization → System collects usage data and available plans
• **Phase 2**: System calculates best plan for each device → Ensures adequate data coverage
• **Phase 3**: Creates Excel report with recommendations → Emails customer with savings summary
• **Customer Gets**: Email notification, detailed spreadsheet, implementation guide
• **Result**: Automatic plan optimization with minimal customer effort

**Real Example**: Logistics company with 250 devices reduced monthly costs from $10,000 to $4,500 (saving $66,000/year)
